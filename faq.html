Common Questions

What most guests ask first.

Here are the most common questions guests usually ask before booking a private fishing charter.

01

Do I need to bring my own fishing rods and reels?

No. Your charter includes premium rod and reel setups, specialized jigs, and trolling lures prepared for the deep channels around Danajon Bank. You may still bring your own personal rod if preferred.

02

Can we bring more than 4 people if some are only watching?

No. The boat is strictly capped at a maximum of 4 guests total, whether they are fishing or spectating. This keeps the experience safe, comfortable, and uncrowded.

03

Are food and drinks provided?

Food and drinks are not provided. Guests should bring their own meals, snacks, and beverages. Ice and cooler support are provided on board.

04

Can we keep the fish we catch?

Yes. The catch belongs to the guests. The crew will help bleed, wash, ice, and store the fish to preserve quality before returning to Hadsan Beach Park.

05

Are photos and videos included?

Yes. The charter includes onboard media documentation using action cameras. Trip footage and photos may be transferred after the session.

06

What happens if the weather is bad?

Guest safety is the priority. If the captain determines that sea or weather conditions are unsafe, guests may choose a priority reschedule date or a refund option.

07

Where is the launch point?

The base of operations is Hadsan Beach Park, Lapu-Lapu City, Mactan, Cebu, Philippines. Guests should arrive at the meeting point before the scheduled departure.

08

When should we arrive?

Guests should arrive 15 minutes before departure. For example, arrive by 4:45 AM for a 5:00 AM morning session.

09

Are accommodations available near the launch point?

Hadsan Beach Park has nearby/on-site amenities that may be useful for early morning departures or late returns from nocturnal sessions.

10

Are alcohol and smoking allowed?

Moderate alcohol consumption may be allowed, but unsafe or impaired behavior can result in the captain terminating the charter. Smoking is restricted for fire safety reasons.

Launch Point

Hadsan Beach Park, Lapu-Lapu City

The charter operates from Mactan’s water edge, helping guests avoid unnecessary mainland travel before the offshore run.

Still Unsure?

Ask before choosing a session.

The right charter depends on your target species, group experience, preferred schedule, and comfort with night or full-day fishing. Send an inquiry and the team will recommend a practical option.
