Fishing Ground Profiles

Different areas, different fish behavior.

01

Caubian Wall & Deep Drop-offs

A productive structure zone for Giant Trevally, Tanguigue, Barracuda, Bigeye Trevally, and night predators.

Techniques: Jigging, trolling, and blue-light night strategy.

02

Outer Barrier Seamounts

Deeper reef and bottom structure where Grouper, Red Snapper, and Katambak may hold.

Techniques: Slow-pitch vertical jigging and bottom work.

03

Northeast Pass

A farther offshore run toward the Camotes Sea border for Yellowfin Tuna, Dogtooth Tuna, Tanguigue, Barracuda, and Ruby Snapper.

Techniques: High-speed trolling, topwater popping, and deep-drop jigging.

04

Danajon Deep Drop

A demanding deep-water profile for trophy Grouper, Snapper, Amberjack, Dogtooth Tuna, and heavy-tackle bottom fishing.

Techniques: Heavy bottom dropping, electric reel support, and slow-pitch work.
