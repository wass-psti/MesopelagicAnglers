Platform Breakdown

Commercial-grade vessel, propulsion, electronics, and lighting.

Every trip is supported by a carefully selected offshore platform designed for safety, stability, deep-water performance, and effective fish-finding capability.

01 The Vessel

Extreme Boats 5.7M Extreme Game King

New Zealand-built Extreme Boats 5.7M Extreme Game King. Heavy-duty aluminum marine hull featuring a deep-V design, flooded keel stability, and a fully enclosed hardtop for absolute safety and a dry ride.

02 The Power

2026 Yamaha F150FETX Four-Stroke Outboard

Powered by a brand new 2026 Yamaha F150FETX Four-Stroke Outboard Motor, offering high-torque output, ultra-quiet idling, and maximum fuel efficiency across deep channels.

It is also equipped with a 2 × 100AH battery system and a 700W solar power generator, enough to power electric reels or allow continuous charging of smartphones onboard.

03 The Eyes & Sonar

Garmin ECHOMAP UHD2 92cv + GT51M-TM Transducer

The vessel uses a Garmin ECHOMAP UHD2 92cv head unit paired with a Garmin GT51M-TM Transom-Mount Transducer.

It pushes 600W of Medium-Band CHIRP (80–160 kHz) and 260 kHz ClearVü/SideVü scanning to read the marine floor down to 1,800 feet or over 500 meters.

This maps underwater seamounts and tracks fish up to 150 meters to the left and right of the hull.

04 Nocturnal Array

400W underwater blue light system

A custom-rigged 400W high-power underwater blue light system cuts through pitch-black waters, pulling in dense bait schools and triggering local feeding frenzies directly beneath the boat deck.

Why It Matters

A fishing platform built for real offshore work.

  • Efficient four-stroke Yamaha power with extended electrical support

  • Night-ready blue-light bait aggregation system
