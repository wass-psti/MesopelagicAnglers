The Eyes

Garmin sonar for structure-led fishing.

The Garmin ECHOMAP UHD2 92cv and GT51M-TM transducer setup supports CHIRP, ClearVü, and SideVü scanning to help read floor structure, seamounts, drop-offs, and fish-holding areas.

  • Garmin ECHOMAP UHD2 92cv head unit
  • Garmin GT51M-TM transducer
  • Medium-band CHIRP support
  • ClearVü and SideVü structure scanning
  • Useful for seamount, channel, and drop-off reading
