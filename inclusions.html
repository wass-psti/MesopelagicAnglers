Charter Inclusions & Policies

Know what is included before you book.

These policies protect guest safety, crew operations, equipment, schedule planning, and the private offshore experience.

Included

What the charter provides.

01

Boat, fuel, and crew support

Private vessel operation, captain/deck support, route planning, and offshore execution are included.

02

Fishing gear and selected tackle

Rod, reel, lure, jig, rigging, and cooler support are prepared depending on route, tier, and target fish.

03

Marine cooler and ice support

Fish and guest provisions can be stored in a large marine cooler with fresh ice during the trip.

04

Media documentation

Basic onboard photo/video documentation may be provided when conditions and crew workload allow.

Policies

Important booking and safety guidelines.

01

Capacity policy

The boat is capped at a maximum of 4 guests total, including anglers, watchers, and photographers.

Capacity graphic placeholder
02

Weather and safety policy

The captain has final go/no-go authority. Trips may be rescheduled or cancelled if sea condition is unsafe.

03

Food, drinks, alcohol, and smoking

Guests bring their own food and drinks. Unsafe, impaired, or disruptive behavior can end the charter.

Guest preparation placeholder
04

Equipment responsibility

Normal lure loss and line breaks are expected, but lost rods or reels due to negligence may require replacement cost.
