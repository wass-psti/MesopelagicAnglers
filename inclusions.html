Inclusions, policies, and trip rules

Everything needed for a serious offshore session.

The charter includes the boat, captain, deckhand, fuel, electronics, premium gear, cooler support, media documentation, and fishing systems. Guests bring their own food and drinks.

BYO Reminder

Bring your own meals, snacks, and drinks.

Food and beverages are not provided. Ice and cooler support are included, so guests can keep their refreshments cold and preserve their catch.

Preparation

What guests should prepare.

The charter handles the fishing platform. Guests should prepare their personal comfort items, food, hydration, and appropriate footwear.

  • Bring meals or heavy snacks suitable for your session length.
  • Bring water, electrolyte drinks, soda, or preferred beverages.
  • Wear non-marking rubber boat shoes or sneakers.
  • Arrive 15 minutes before departure at Hadsan Beach Park.
  • Bring sun protection for daytime sessions.
  • Bring extra clothes or wind protection for night sessions.
Booking Policy

Reservation and safety rules.

Deposit

50% Reservation Deposit

A 50% reservation deposit is required after date and session confirmation. The remaining balance is settled before boarding.

Capacity

Maximum 4 Guests

The boat is capped at 4 guests total, including anyone who only wants to watch or take photos.

Arrival

15 Minutes Early

Guests should arrive at the Hadsan Beach Park meeting point 15 minutes before departure.

Cancellation and Weather

Safety stays above schedule.

Client Cancellation

Cancellation Summary

  • 100% refund if cancelled 7 days or more before departure.
  • 50% deposit forfeiture if cancelled 2 to 6 days before departure.
  • 100% deposit forfeiture within 48 hours or in case of no-show.
Weather Policy

Captain-Led Safety Decision

If the captain determines sea or weather conditions are unsafe, guests may choose between a priority reschedule date or a 100% refund.
