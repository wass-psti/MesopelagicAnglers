Fleet, Electronics & Tackle

Commercial-grade platform. Premium sportfishing equipment.

Mesopelagic Anglers uses a dedicated offshore setup built around safety, stability, sonar visibility, power support, night operations, and serious fishing capability.

The Vessel

Extreme Boats 5.7M Extreme Game King

A New Zealand-built heavy-duty aluminum marine hull with a deep-V design, flooded keel stability, and fully enclosed hardtop for a safer, drier offshore ride.

The Power

2026 Yamaha F150FETX Four-Stroke Outboard Motor

Provides high-torque output, quiet operation, and efficient power for offshore channel runs.

On-board electrical support

  • 2 x 100AH battery system
  • 700W solar power generator
  • Support for electric reels
  • Smartphone charging capability on board

The Eyes & Sonar

Garmin ECHOMAP UHD2 92cv + Garmin GT51M-TM Transducer

The sonar setup is used to read underwater structure, locate drop-offs, identify seamounts, and track fish-holding zones.

  • Medium-band CHIRP support
  • ClearVü scanning
  • SideVü scanning
  • Deep structure reading capability

Nocturnal Array

400W High-Power Underwater Blue Lights

Used during night sessions to attract bait schools and help trigger predatory feeding activity beneath and around the boat.

Elite Tackle Armory

Every charter includes access to premium heavy-duty tackle setups paired with tournament-grade rods.

Shimano Beast Master 6000

Electric reel setup intended for deep-water targets such as Ruby Snapper and Grouper from significant depths.

Shimano Talica 50II

A two-speed lever drag reel suited for offshore trolling and heavy pelagic fights.

Daiwa Saltiga 35N & Saltiga LD35

Overhead reel setups for mechanical and high-speed vertical jigging.

Bullzen Anunaki 50N

Narrow spool jigging reel designed for heavy braid and strong drag pressure against reef predators.

Cannon Uni-Troll 5 ST Downrigger

Depth-control system used to position trolling lures in target strike zones, especially for Tanguigue and pelagic fish staging at specific depth bands.
