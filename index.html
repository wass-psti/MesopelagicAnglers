Quick answers for common booking questions. Keep this section short and direct; the detailed follow-up can happen through Monday after inquiry submission.

View Full FAQ

Do I need to bring my own rods and reels?

No. Premium rods, reels, jigs, and trolling lures are included with the charter.

Can we bring more than 4 people?

No. Capacity is strictly capped at 4 guests total for safety, comfort, and deck space.

Are food and drinks included?

Food and drinks are Bring-Your-Own. Ice and cooler support are provided on board.

What happens if weather is unsafe?

The captain makes the safety decision. Guests may receive a priority reschedule or refund option.
