Tier 1 Premium

The Middle Pass & Caubian Islands

An exclusive journey to the Danajon Bank, one of only six double barrier reefs on Earth. Crossing the Middle Pass by the Caubian Islands, this tier drops lines into rare underwater topography where massive current breaks create a haven for game fish.

Package Profile

  • Capacity: Maximum 4 anglers
  • Available Sessions: Morning, Afternoon, Nocturnal Night, or Full-Day Marathon
  • Session Lengths: 6 hours, 12 hours, or 14 hours depending on selected charter window
  • Rates: Rates vary by daytime or nocturnal sessions. Send us a DM at Mesopelagic Anglers for a fully tailored private quote.

Main Fishing Profile

Tier 1 is built for private groups that want a premium reef-edge and current-break fishing route without pushing as far into the longer-range offshore grounds. It focuses on productive Danajon Bank structure, Caubian-side drop-offs, and moving-water zones where pelagic and reef predators actively feed.

  • Mamsa / Giant Trevally
  • Tanguigue / Spanish Mackerel
  • Barracuda
  • Bigeye Trevally
  • Lapu-Lapu / Trophy Grouper
  • Ahaan / Red Snapper
  • Katambak / Longnose Emperor
  • Yellowfin Tuna
Targeted Fishing Grounds

Where Tier 1 focuses its fishing effort.

01

The Caubian Wall & Deep Drop-offs

Daytime Targets: Aggressive Mamsa / Giant Trevally, trophy Tanguigue / Spanish Mackerel, and large Barracuda.

Nighttime Targets: Schooling Bigeye Trevally and predatory Barracuda feeding under the blue lights.

Techniques: Shallow-water jigging and high-speed trolling.

02

The Outer Barrier Seamounts

Targets: Deep-dwelling Lapu-Lapu / Trophy Grouper, Ahaan / Red Snapper, and Katambak / Longnose Emperor.

Techniques: Slow-pitch vertical jigging.

03

The Middle Pass Drift

Targets: Schooling jacks and migrating Yellowfin Tuna.

Techniques: Light-tackle casting or shallow-water trolling.

Tier 2 Deluxe

The Deep Blue Danajon Banks

Our ultimate long-range expedition for experienced anglers and thrill-seekers looking for an adrenaline rush. We leave Mactan far behind to target massive offshore sportfish, deep-water reef monsters, and heavy pelagics in the untamed channels of the Visayan Sea.

Package Profile

  • Capacity: Maximum 4 anglers
  • Available Sessions: Morning, Afternoon, Nocturnal Night, or Full-Day Marathon
  • Session Lengths: 6 hours, 12 hours, or 14 hours depending on selected charter window
  • Rates: Exclusive custom packages are available for serious corporate groups and sportfishing teams. Drop us a message for current seasonal booking rates.

Main Fishing Profile

Tier 2 is the deeper, longer-range expedition for experienced anglers who want heavier offshore action. It is designed for larger pelagics, deep-water reef predators, and more demanding fishing grounds beyond the regular Mactan-side charter route.

  • Yellowfin Tuna
  • Dogtooth Tuna
  • Tanguigue / Spanish Mackerel
  • Barracuda
  • Ruby Snapper
  • Mamsa / Giant Trevally
  • Amberjack
  • Lapu-Lapu / Grouper
  • Ahaan / Red Snapper
Targeted Fishing Grounds

Where Tier 2 pushes farther offshore.

01

The Northeast Pass

Route Area: Camotes Sea border.

Targets: Yellowfin Tuna, Dogtooth Tuna, Tanguigue / Spanish Mackerel, Barracuda, and deep-dwelling Ruby Snapper.

Techniques: High-speed trolling, topwater popping, and deep-drop jigging.

02

The Caubian Middle Pass

Daytime Targets: Trophy Mamsa / Giant Trevally, Amberjack, and passing Yellowfin Tuna.

Nighttime Targets: Heavy shallow-water jigging for massive Bigeye Trevally and reef-patrolling Barracuda.

03

The Danajon Deep Drop

Targets: Trophy-sized Lapu-Lapu / Grouper, giant Ahaan / Red Snapper, Dogtooth Tuna, and Amberjack.

Techniques: Slow-pitch vertical jigging and heavy-tackle bottom dropping with electric reels.
